# An Explainable Artificial Intelligence Framework for Medical Diagnosis

## Project Overview

This repository contains the research project titled **"An Explainable Artificial Intelligence Framework for Medical Diagnosis"** developed as part of academic research work.

The project focuses on addressing the limitations of traditional black-box Artificial Intelligence systems used in healthcare. Although conventional AI models achieve strong predictive performance, they often fail to explain how decisions are generated. This lack of transparency reduces clinician trust and limits real-world adoption.

To overcome these limitations, the proposed framework integrates **Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI)** techniques such as **SHAP** and **LIME** to provide diagnostic predictions along with understandable explanations. The framework aims to improve **interpretability, transparency, clinician trust, and decision validation** while maintaining reliable predictive performance.
